About

Hi - I'm Marianne Menglin Liu, a Principal Applied Scientist on the Generative AI Science team at Oracle AI (OCI), where I work on natural language interfaces for enterprise AI and data platforms.

I focus on industrial AI research, building production-grade systems that connect large language models with enterprise data and tools. My work centers on NL2SQL, agentic reasoning, and data-centric AI, with an emphasis on evaluation, reliability, and real-world deployment.

At Oracle, I've worked across agent development, evaluation, and data generation, and was part of the team that won the NL2SQL Archer Challenge in 2025. Previously, I was a Senior Data Scientist at IBM, where I built clinical-driven AI systems for healthcare and pharmaceutical applications.

I hold a Master's in Information Systems from Carnegie Mellon University and a bachelor's degree from Fudan University, with an interdisciplinary background in social sciences that informs my approach to real-world AI problems.
